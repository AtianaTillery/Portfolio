Hello! I'm Atiana.

I could list off my professional skills like adaptability, problem-solving, and tech expertise... but I'd rather start by telling you who I am. I’m the youngest in my family — and the only girl. Growing up surrounded entirely by brothers and male cousins meant I had no choice but to be tough, outspoken, and unapologetically myself. That environment taught me early how to stand out and hold my own, and it’s shaped the way I move through life — with confidence, creativity, and drive.

I found my love for coding when I realized just how powerful it is to bring ideas to life. There’s something deeply satisfying about solving problems through code — writing a line, running it, tweaking it, and watching it evolve into something real and functional. I’ve built mobile and web-based apps using technologies like Python, Java, Flutter, and HTML/CSS. One of my proudest experiences so far has been developing an anatomy learning app during my internship. It helped me realize how much I enjoy building things that people can actually use — following a blueprint, translating it into clean, functional code, and watching it become something that meets a real need.

Outside of tech, I’m big on staying active and connected to the things that ground me. You can usually find me at a car event with friends, in the gym lifting, playing soccer, or on a hike with my two Japanese Akitas — Casanova and Yami.
